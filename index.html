Starknet Provisions

The next step toward decentralization


The Starknet Foundation is proud to introduce Provisions! Members of various groups who have helped advance Starknet, including Starknet users and builders, Ethereum contributors and stakers, and builders of select projects, may claim provisions.Check below to see if you are eligible

Are you eligible for STRK tokens?

Starknet users

You used Starknet more than five times in total during three seperate months or more, transacted with a cumulative amount of at least $100 before November 15th, 2023, and had 0.005 ETH or more in your account on that date.

Starknet community

You are an approved participant of the Early Community Member Program (ECMP).

Starknet developers

You made at least three commits to a repository that appears in the Electric Capital report for Starknet before November 15th, 2023. At least one of these commits occurred during 2018 or later.

StarkEx users

StarkEx users

You used a StarkEx-based app at least eight times before June 1st, 2022.

Ethereum developers

Ethereum developers

EIP authors

Ethereum stakers

You staked ETH up until the Merge - September 15th, 2022. Note that if you staked via a staking pool or centralized exchange you can't claim ETH directly through the portal. Your provider should claim the allocated STRK and distribute it to you.

GitHub developers

Check your eligibility

Use one of the methods below to check if you are eligible for the Starknet Provisions Program and, if you are, how much STRK you have been allocated

Connect your Starknet wallet

Eligibility criteria

You are a consistent Starknet user, or an approved participant of ECMP.

or

Sign in with StarkEx

Eligibility criteria

If you used a StarkEx product prior to Jun 01, 2022

Connect your Ethereum wallet

Eligibility criteria

You are a member of the Ethereum Protocol Guild, or you staked ETH directly from Ethereum before the Merge. If you staked ETH through an exchange or a liquidity pool, contact them to check your eligibility.

or

Sign in with GitHub

Eligibility criteria

You are a consistent contributor to a Starknet-related, Ethereum-related, or a GitHub top-ranked repository, or a contributor to the EIP repository.
